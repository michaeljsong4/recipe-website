Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients

  • boneless skinless chicken breast
  • white rice
  • frozen broccoli
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • maple syrup
  • garlic powder
  • ground ginger

Cooking Directions

  1. Season chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and ground ginger
  2. Air Fry chicken at 400 degrees Fahrenheit for 18 minutes
  3. Prepare white rice with rice cooker

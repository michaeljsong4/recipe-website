Chili

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 can dark red kidney beans (Rinsed and drained)
  • 1 can light red kidney beans (Rinsed and drained)
  • 1 diced yellow onion
  • 6 cloves of diced garlic
  • 24oz Tomoto Sauce
  • 2x 14oz cans of Diced Tomatoes (undrained)
  • garlic powder
  • chili powder
  • 1/2 a can of beef broth
  • shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Cooking Directions

  1. Brown Ground Beef in frying pan
  2. Combine ingredients in slow cooker
  3. Slow cook on low for 6-7 hours
  4. Top with shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  5. Enjoy!
